Assignment Deliverables / 課題成果物

Deliverable 1: Classification Criteria Document

成果物1：分類基準文書

Required Content / 必要な内容:

Document your explicit criteria for classifying companies as "Interested" (1) or "Not Interested" (0).

企業を「興味あり」(1)または「興味なし」(0)に分類するための明示的な基準を文書化します。

Include the following / 以下を含める:

  1. Industry Preferences / 業界の好み

    Which industries do you prefer? Which do you want to avoid?

    どの業界を好みますか？どれを避けたいですか？

  2. Company Size Preferences / 企業規模の好み

    Do you prefer large corporations, mid-size companies, or startups?

    大企業、中規模企業、またはスタートアップを好みますか？

  3. Location Preferences / 所在地の好み

    Do you have location preferences? Remote work important?

    所在地の好みはありますか？リモートワークは重要ですか？

  4. Keyword Indicators / キーワード指標

    What keywords suggest an interesting company to you?

    どのキーワードがあなたにとって興味深い企業を示唆しますか？

    Examples: "innovation", "global", "AI", "sustainability"

  5. Other Important Factors / その他の重要な要因

    Any other criteria that matter to you?

    あなたにとって重要な他の基準はありますか？

Format / 形式:

  • Document format: PDF, Word, or Google Doc
  • Length: 1-2 pages
  • Clear sections for each criterion type

Scoring (20 points) / 採点（20点）:

  • Clear, specific criteria: 10 points
  • Personally relevant to career interests: 5 points
  • Consistently applicable: 5 points

Deliverable 3: Label Analysis Report

成果物3：ラベル分析レポート

Tips for Success / 成功のためのヒント

Defining Good Criteria / 良い基準の定義

  • ✅ Be specific: "Tech companies with AI focus" not just "good companies"
  • ✅ Consider multiple factors: industry, size, location, keywords
  • ✅ Write criteria BEFORE labeling to maintain consistency
  • ✅ Ask yourself: "Can I apply this consistently to all companies?"

Labeling Process / ラベリングプロセス

  • ✅ Take your time - quality matters more than speed
  • ✅ Label in one sitting if possible (for consistency)
  • ✅ Trust your gut on borderline cases
  • ✅ Document why you're unsure about difficult cases

Achieving Balance / バランスの達成

  • ✅ Aim for 30-70% split (closer to 50-50 is ideal)
  • ✅ If too imbalanced, revisit your criteria
  • ✅ Consider: Are you being too picky or too lenient?

Common Issues & Solutions / 一般的な問題と解決策

Problem: All companies seem interesting (or all uninteresting)

Solution: Refine your criteria to be more selective. Compare companies directly: "If I could only work at ONE, which would I choose?" Remember: "not interested" doesn't mean the company is bad.

Problem: Can't decide on borderline cases

Solution: Trust your gut feeling. If genuinely unsure after considering criteria, flip a coin! Borderline cases can go either way - consistency matters more than perfection.

Problem: Labels seem inconsistent when reviewing

Solution: Review your criteria and adjust if needed. It's okay to relabel companies. If similar companies have different labels, reconsider one of them.

Problem: Very imbalanced labels (e.g., 22 interested, 3 not)

Solution: Options: (1) Be more selective, (2) Check if your dataset has enough variety, (3) Proceed with imbalance (ML can still work - just note it). Don't force unnatural labels.
